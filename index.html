Sindhutai Sapkal (Mai)

'Mother of Orphans'

Sou. Sindhutai Sapkal also known as Mother of Orphans is an Indian social worker and social activist known particularly for her work for raising orphan children.

MISSION

To bring up the orphans, destitute, distressed, children and women rehabilitation and provide them with basic necessities. To uplift the deprived section of the society and make them capable to live as respected human being.

ACHIEVEMENTS

40 years of continuous Social Works, More Than 750 Awards, Mother of more than 1050 orphaned children, 207 Sons-in-law, 36 daughters-in-law

AWARDS

Sou. Sindhutai Sapkal has been honoured with over 272 awards for her dedication and work including 2010 - Ahilyabai Holkar Award given by the Government of Maharashtra to social workers in the field of woman and child welfare

LEGACY

The 2010 marathi film "Mee Sindhutai Sapkal" by Anant Mahadevan is a bio-pic inspired by the true story of Sindhutai Sapkal.
